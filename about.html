Vission

U Liva Nutrition LLP was established with the thought of “Supporting healthy living globally”. We are a young, qualified, enthusiastic and passionate team with one committed to making this world healthier.

At U Liva Nutrition, we take pride to discover, develop and manufacture trustworthy nutritional supplements products based on the latest scientific research studies in GMP affirmed plants. Products are developed using scientifically validated ingredients and scientifically supported doses. The company has a broad portfolio of herbal supplements and food supplements products.

Our product line is chosen with the intent to help improve the general well-being and health of our clients. Our products are uniquely selected to represent those herbs that have proven their health benefits from hundreds of years of Ayurveda wisdom.

Mission

At U Liva Nutrition, we provide value-added Nutritional supplement products & services for the full satisfaction of our customer’s needs.

Our mission is to use our expertise, professionalism, and dedication to offer better quality and cost-effective health supplement products and services to our clients. We are also dedicated to continuous investment to improve our technical advantage through scientific research and global technical cooperation to better serve our customers.
